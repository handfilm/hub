The Q/W/E Engine
HANDS NEVER
LEAVE HOME.

The entire cut architecture rests on three adjacent keys. Q marks the in-point. W marks the out-point. E executes the extraction. Your right hand controls playback. Your left hand never moves.

This isn't a preference — it's a production philosophy. When the mechanical friction of editing drops to zero, the only thing left is rhythm. That's where the content lives.

The PDF hotkey sheet included in every tier is the cheat code. Print it, tape it to your desk, internalize it inside a single session.

Q
→ Mark In-Point (Top)
W
→ Mark Out-Point (Tail)
E
→ Extract & Ripple Delete
SPACE
→ Play / Pause
J
→ Rewind
L
→ Fast Forward
