Capsule 005 — Engineered Indigo

Built For Mechanical Stretch

Engineered Indigo is denim run through the same tension tests as our activewear line. Warp and weft are selected for mechanical stretch, not just comfort stretch — the difference is a fabric that pulls back into spec after every wear, not one that quietly gives up by week three.

Zero-sag recovery is built into the yarn, not patched in with a lining. Every wash is raw factory wash — no chemical shortcuts, no synthetic fade sprayed on after the fact. What fades, fades because it was actually worn.

01

Heavyweight Recovery

A denim weight dense enough to hold its shape through a full day of wear — engineered so the knee and seat never bag out, regardless of fit.

02

Industrial Wash

Raw fading achieved through mechanical abrasion and rope-dye process, not chemical bleaching. Every crease reads true because it was earned, not printed.
